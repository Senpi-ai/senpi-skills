# Discovery Guide Reference

Use this when running **Step 2: Discovery** of the first-trade tutorial. This step is about **finding top traders and recommending one to mirror** (not opening individual asset/direction positions). Use **`discovery_get_top_traders`**; optionally **`discovery_get_trader_state`** or **`discovery_get_trader_history`** to confirm a trader has open positions. **Only present as options traders that currently have open positions** â€” do not offer traders with no open positions. **Show the user only user-friendly copy;** do not mention state, step names, or MCP/tool names.

## MCP Usage

- **`discovery_get_top_traders`** â€” Fetch top traders (e.g. by PnL, win rate, or consistency).
- **Filter to traders with open positions** â€” Only show options for traders that **currently have open positions**. Use **`discovery_get_trader_state`** (or equivalent) to confirm open positions before including a trader in the list. Do not offer traders with no open positions.
- Optionally **`discovery_get_trader_history`** â€” Get extra detail for a specific trader before recommending.
- Prefer traders whose positions are in liquid assets (ETH, BTC, SOL) so the mirror strategy has smooth entry/exit.

---

## What to Look For

- **Open positions** â€” Only include traders that **currently have open positions** (so the mirror strategy has something to copy).
- **Strong performance** â€” Among those, prefer top traders by PnL, win rate, or consistency.
- **Liquid assets** â€” Traders with positions in ETH, BTC, SOL are better for a first mirror strategy.
- **Recent activity** â€” Traders with recent activity so the mirror has clear positions to follow.

