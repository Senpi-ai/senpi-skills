### Lessons Learned (cumulative)
1. Don't cap winners — no native TP in Phase 1
2. Trailing stops > fixed TP for momentum trades
3. `edit_position` is the Swiss Army knife (add, reduce, SL, TP)
4. Test momentum calcs with real data (null bug hid for hours)
5. 1h trend filter is critical — counter-trend entries = guaranteed losses
6. 5min SL sync delay prevents instant death on entry noise
7. Time decay should be lenient on slow-developing trends
8. Fees ($1.12/trade) are irrelevant vs one good trend capture ($50-80)
9. Auto-adopt orphan positions — never leave a position unmanaged
10. Be autonomous — don't ask permission, act on data
